Frequently Asked Questions

To schedule an appointment, navigate to the "Appointments" section and click "Schedule New". Select your provider, preferred date and time, and reason for visit. You'll receive a confirmation once your appointment is booked.

Lab results are available in the "Lab Reports" section. New results will be marked with a "New" badge. Click on any report to view detailed results. Some sensitive results may require provider approval before being visible.

Go to the "Prescriptions" section and locate the medication you need refilled. Click the "Request Refill" button. Your request will be sent to your provider for approval. You'll be notified once the refill is ready.

Navigate to the "Telehealth" section 5 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. Click "Join Call" when your appointment appears. Ensure your camera and microphone are working. You'll enter a virtual waiting room until your provider joins.

Go to "Settings" and then "Personal Details". Make any necessary changes to your information and click "Update Profile". Note that some demographic information may require verification with our staff.
